3.2 Dynamics' indicators

Indicators of dynamics are affixes that indicate whether the noun is .

noun prefix
stative NST the noun is not changing its state or form -

3.3 Noun declension - cases

case description suffix
nominative NOM marks the subject of a verb -
genitive GEN marks a possessor
dative DAT marks the indirect object, typically the recipient of the action
accusative ACC marks the direct object of a verb
instrumental INS marks the means or instrument used to perform the action
allative ALL indicates movement towards a place
ablative ABL indicates movement away from a place
locative LOC indicates location or position
